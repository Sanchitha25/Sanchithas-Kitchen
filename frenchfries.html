French Fries

French Fries requires minimal amount of time as well as ingredients to make it. It is one of the easiest and quick snacks which you can make with very minimal ingredients.

prep time

15 min

cook time

15 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Firstly take 2 potatoes and peel it's skin off and slice the potato in stick shape.

step 2

Rinse these potato sticks for a couple of times in water and after 30 minutes or 45 minutes, drain the potatoes in a colander.

step 3

Then place them on a clean kitchen cotton napkin. Fold the napkin over to press, pat and dry the potatoes. The potatoes have to be completely dried before you begin to fry them.

step 4

Heat oil in a pan over medium or medium-low heat. Now, working in batches, add the potatoes to the oil. Stir in intervals while frying them for uniform cooking.

step 5

Fry in oil until the potatoes are cooked, but not brown from outside. They should just get a crust from outside without any major color change. Light browning of the edges is fine.

step 6

Place the half fried potatoes on paper kitchen towels. Stack 3 to 4 paper towels. Press some paper towels from top also to absorb extra oil. Let the half fried potatoes cool down at room temperature.

step 7

Now increase the flame to a medium-high or high and fry french fries for 3 minutes or until crisp and golden. The edges should become golden.

step 8

Remove finger chips with a slotted spoon. Place french fries on paper tissues again to remove the extra oil.

step 9

While still hot, take the french fries in a bowl. Sprinkle with some salt and red chili flakes. Gently shake and toss the bowl so that the salt, herbs etc mix well with the finger chips.

step 10

Serve french fries hot with any dip or sauce of your choice.

ingredients

oil

2 potatoes

salt

chilli flakes
