Beans Palya

Beans Palya requires minimal amount of time as well as ingredients to make it. It can used as a side dish for chapathi, dosa, roti and other main course. Also, it can be served as a side dish for rice and sambar.

prep time

15 min

cook time

15 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

In a cooker, add oil, mustard seeds, chana dal, curry leaves, green chillies, chopped onions and fry till onion becomes light brown.

step 2

To this, add chopped beans and carrot, salt to taste and mix it well.

step 3

To this, add half cup water and let cooker whistle for 2 whistles.

step 4

Top it with coriander leaves and coconut grate and serve hot with your main course.

ingredients

2tbspn oil

1tbspn mustard seeds

1tbspn chana dal

1tbspn curry leaves

2 green chillies

1 onion

1 cup beans

1 cup carrot

1tbspn salt

1/2 cup water

1/2 cup coriander leaves

1/2 cup coconut grate
