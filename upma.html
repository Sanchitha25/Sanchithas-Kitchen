Upma

Upma is one of the easiest and quickest breakfast dish you can make. It is one of the quickest breakfast preferred in every indian household.

prep time

15 min

cook time

15 min

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

In a pan, add oil and fry rava until it leaves raw smell and turns light brown colour and transfer it to a separate vessel.

step 2

In same pan, add oil ,mustard seeds, chana dal, curry leaves, green chillies, chopped onions and fry until onion turns golden brown in colour.

step 3

To this, add water, salt to taste and let water to boil.

step 4

When water is boiling, add the fried rava(water:rava=3:1) to it. Close the lid and let it boil.

step 5

Once it is boiled, add lemon juice, coriander leaves, coconut grate and mix everything well. Upma is ready to be served.

ingredients

oil

1tbspn mustard seeds

1tbspn chana dal

1tbspn curry leaves

4 green chillies

2 onion

1 cup rava

3 cup water

salt to taste

1tbspn lemon juice

1/2 cup coriander leaves

1/2 cup coconut grate
