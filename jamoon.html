jamoon

Jamoon is a very quick and easy sweet which you can make. It is one of the preferred sweet in eevry indian household. In our recipe, we are using the ready MTS Gulab Jamoon mix to make jamoon.

prep time

15 min

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

In a pan, first prepare sugar syrup by adding sugar, water, cardamom, kesari colour.

step 2

In a bowl, add MTR gulab jamoon mix and milk. Mix everything well and prepare a dough. Rest it for 15 mins.

step 3

After 15 mins, make small balls from the prepared dough.

step 4

Fry it in oil until it is golden brown and add this to prepared sugar syrup and let it soak for half an hour.

step 5

After it is soaked for half an hour, gulab jamoon is ready to be served hot.

ingredients

oil

1/2 cup sugar

3 cup water

3 cardamom

1tbspn kesari colour

milk

MTR Gulab jamoon mix
